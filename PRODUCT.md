# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Indonesian people managing money together. The primary surface is shared rooms:
household members and small groups splitting and tracking expenses, seeing who
paid for what, watching a group budget. The same people also run a personal
core (their own transactions, budgets, receipts). Usage is mobile, Android
first, on the go, often one handed mid task (at the warung, after a transfer,
before bed). Solo logging is the daily driver; rooms are the layer that makes
it social.

## Product Purpose

LOIT lets Indonesians log spending and keep shared money honest without
friction. Success is the user opening the app, recording what happened in
seconds, and trusting the numbers, then closing it. The product wins on the
boring repeated act of capture, not on dashboards. Shared rooms turn private
tracking into a low drama group ledger.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, lively, local. The voice is a money smart friend, not a bank.
Indonesian consumer warmth: approachable, a little playful, culturally
grounded, in Bahasa Indonesia first. Lively does not mean loud. It respects
that this is real money and real relationships.

## Anti-references

- **Cold corporate banking.** Navy and gold legacy bank apps, dense forms,
  jargon, intimidation. LOIT is the opposite of a teller window.
- **Crypto and fintech hype.** Neon on black, aggressive gradients, gamified
  greed, hype copy, fake urgency. No moon, no FOMO.
- **Cluttered spreadsheet dashboards.** Walls of tiny numbers, chart overload,
  everything crammed on one screen. Clarity beats density every time.

## Design Principles

- **Capture is the hot path.** Logging a transaction is the act users repeat
  most. It must feel instant and forgettable. Optimize the common case
  ruthlessly; push everything else out of the way.
- **Shared money is social, not just numeric.** In rooms, who paid and who owes
  is as important as the amount. Make people and provenance legible, keep it
  low drama.
- **Warm, never corporate.** Lean into local friendly over sterile bank
  authority. The user should feel met by a friend, not processed by an
  institution.
- **Lively but it is real money.** Personality and delight are welcome; anxiety,
  hype, and toy-like cartoonishness are not. Calm confidence under the warmth.
- **Clarity over density.** Show the few numbers that matter now. Never a
  spreadsheet. Progressive depth for users who want more.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast as the baseline (the design system already retones brand for
dark mode and AA on white). Localized Bahasa Indonesia and English (`app_id`,
`app_en`), Indonesian first. Legible at small sizes and one handed reach on
phones. No additional special accommodations committed at this time.
